# Nine AI Methods for Auteur Cinema

*By Ismaël Joffroy Chandoutis*

---

Nine methods developed through practice — not theory — for using AI in documentary and post-documentary filmmaking. Each method solves a specific production problem. None of them are about "AI filmmaking" as a genre. They are about using available tools to make the film that needs to be made.

---

## Methods

1. [**Spatial Reconstruction**](spatial-reconstruction.md) — Rebuilding spaces that cannot be filmed, from photogrammetry to AI-assisted 3D reconstruction.

2. [**3D-Guided Image Generation (ControlNet)**](controlnet-3d-guided.md) — Using Blender scenes as spatial anchors for AI-generated images. Structure from 3D, texture from diffusion.

3. [**Blender → ComfyUI Real-Time Pipeline**](blender-comfyui-realtime.md) — Live rendering pipeline connecting 3D environments to diffusion models in real-time.

4. [**Custom Model Fine-Tuning (LoRA)**](lora-custom-training.md) — Training compact models that know specific locations, characters, and visual vocabularies.

5. [**Cinematic Still Image Generation**](sd-cinematic-image.md) — Generating photographic images with cinematic qualities: lighting, depth of field, composition.

6. [**Latent Drift Animation**](latent-drift-animation.md) — Animation through continuous traversal of latent space, producing movement that is neither filmed nor keyframed.

7. [**Archive Animation**](animation-archives.md) — Bringing archival material to life through AI-assisted animation techniques.

8. [**Image Upscaling**](image-upscaling.md) — Restoring and enhancing degraded source material for cinema-quality output.

9. [**LLM Corpus Fine-Tuning for Living Subjects**](llm-corpus-avatar.md) — Fine-tuning language models on a specific person's documented speech and writing. The most ethically complex method in this collection.

---

*These methods are part of [deep-research](https://github.com/12georgiadis/deep-research), a collection of investigations into the tools, systems, and ideas shaping AI-native creative practice.*
